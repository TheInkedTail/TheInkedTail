Meet Michael

Michael Garcia is a fantasy and speculative fiction author whose work delves into the rich, strange, and sometimes unsettling corners of storytelling. With a love for character-driven narratives and intricate worldbuilding, his stories often explore themes of identity, loss, resilience, and redemption.

Raised on adventure, tabletop games, and mythical stories, Michael’s early fascination with storytelling evolved into a full-fledged craft. He draws inspiration from many games, stories, and personal experiences to create thoroughly developed worlds and stories.

When he’s not writing, Michael is usually expanding on world building, enjoying story rich games, or connecting with readers. He believes stories aren’t just entertainment — they’re a window into a world, new experiences, and ways to connect with like-minded people.

Michael currently publishes serialized fiction on Royal Road, with two growing titles under his belt and more to come.
